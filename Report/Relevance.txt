Key Research Questions

1. Reconstruction Fidelity
How accurately can we reconstruct missing channels?
Does accuracy degrade non-linearly as channels reduce?

2. Direction Matters?
Is 19 → 5 → 19 worse than 19 → 10 → 19?

3. Spatial Importance
Are some electrodes more “informative” than others?

4. Task Impact
Does reconstruction preserve:
    Frequency bands (alpha, beta, etc.)
Event-related potentials (ERP)
Classification performance (e.g., seizure detection)
